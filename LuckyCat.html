The basics: Why LuckyCat?

LuckyCat features industry standard tokenomics and a veteran team of crypto developers. Our verified contract employs both an auto-liquidity mechanism and a reflection fee on all transactions. The days of complicated yield farming, calculating APY, and impermanent loss are over. With LuckyCat, you can simply relax and watch your investment grow. Me-ow!

4% Reflection Fee

Embrace those diamond hands. LuckyCat reflects 4% of every transaction back to every holder. Need we say more?

4% Liquidity Fee

We value liquidity. Thats why we ensure that another 4% of every transaction is funneled to our liquidity pool. A liquid investor is a happy investor.

3% Community Fee

A small fee on every transaction funds our marketing, community building, giveaways, and developer buyback operations. None of this would be possible without your support.

Locked liquidity

Liquidity will be locked, meaning you can rest easy knowing that your money isnt going anywhere.

community focus

We know that without the trust of our community, we wont make it past the atmosphere. Thats why were committed to giving back to our investors through giveaways, contests, a customized DApp, and more.
